Got Questions?

Answers, clearly.

How exactly does the NFC display work?

+
Your iPhone uses NFC (Near Field Communication) to send an image to the e-ink panel inside the case. Open our free Nexly app, choose a design, then tap your iPhone against the case. In under 2 seconds, the new image is locked in — no Bluetooth pairing, no Wi-Fi needed, no electricity required after the transfer.

Does the case need to be charged?

+
Never. E-ink technology only uses power during the image refresh. Once your design is set, it stays on display for months without consuming any energy. The case has no battery, no charging port, nothing to plug in.

Can I use my own photos and designs?

+
Absolutely. Upload personal photos, your own artwork, custom text, or memes through the free Nexly app. You can also browse thousands of professionally designed templates in our library — updated weekly.

Is it compatible with MagSafe and wireless charging?

+
Yes. Nexly works perfectly with MagSafe accessories and Qi wireless chargers. Charge your iPhone normally — the case stays on.
